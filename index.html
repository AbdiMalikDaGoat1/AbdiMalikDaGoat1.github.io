My name is Abdi. I'm a 9th grader at ESUMS from London. I like building things and creating things, whether it's art, science, or whatever project comes my way.

The Mission

This site has two projects on it. They look different on the outside, but they came from the same place. Both started with a question we could not answer right away. Both ended with me learning something I did not expect. I am not trying to give easy answers here. I just want to show how I worked through hard questions and what I found after.

My Screenprinting Project

This started as an art assignment, but it turned into something personal. The goal was to shed light on a social issue. After a lot of back and forth, I landed on something close to home, the way we stop noticing the beautiful places we live in. I drew the design in Photoshop, built the screen by hand, and printed it myself. To learn more, click below.

My DNA Project

This project was about finding out who we actually matched with using our own DNA. My team and I went from collecting cheek swabs to running gel electrophoresis to get our final results. Along the way, we learned something bigger than just the science. We learned what DNA can and cannot tell you about a person and about where they came from. To learn more, click below.
