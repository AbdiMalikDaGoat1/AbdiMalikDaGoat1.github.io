Who I Am

My name is Abdi. I grew up in London. I am a 9th-grade student, and I like turning ideas into something people can actually see. I like taking a thought from my head and finding a way to make it real. Sometimes that means art. Sometimes that means science. Either way, I want to figure it out with my own hands.

The Mission

This site has two projects on it. They look different on the outside, but they came from the same place. Both started with a question I could not answer right away. Both ended with me learning something I did not expect. I am not trying to give easy answers here. I just want to show how I worked through hard questions and what I found after.

My Screenprinting Project

This started as an art assignment, but it turned into something personal. The goal was to shed light on a social issue. After a lot of back and forth, I landed on something close to home, the way we stop noticing the beautiful places we live in. I drew the design in Photoshop, built the screen by hand, and printed it myself. To learn more, click below.

My DNA Project

This project was about finding out who we actually matched with using our own DNA. My team and I went from collecting cheek swabs to running gel electrophoresis to get our final results.
